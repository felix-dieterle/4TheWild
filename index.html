📍 Location

🗺 Plan a Trip

Register your intention to visit the current map area. Other users planning the same area will see a lower silence score, helping everyone avoid unintentional overcrowding.

🧭 Navigation

Enter a destination and select your travel mode to calculate a route from your current location (or the map centre).

🚌 Transportation

Select how you travel to determine which areas are reachable. Spots more than 200 m from any accessible route will be greyed out on the map and excluded from the quietest-spots list. Select multiple modes to combine reachability.

Analyze Area

Pan & zoom the map to your region of interest, then click Analyze to compute the noise heatmap.
